About the Prophet

Prophet Francis Kwateng, founder and leader of House of Power Ministries International, is known for his truthfulness and righteousness. Prophet Francis Kwateng is a messenger from Heaven who is tasked to come for the sons of God and take them to Heaven. “ Awurade se me mmefa ne mma mmere no Heaven ”. Prophet Francis Kwateng. He also said, “ For as many who are the sons of God cannot be snatched out of my hands ”.
Mrs. Mercy Kwateng, wife of Prophet Francis Kwateng, together with Prophet Francis Kwateng founded the church, House of Power Ministries International in Takyiman in small school, but with time and truthfulness, has eventually become the biggest church in the country with the prior vision of taking people to Heaven. As a result, the multimedia was formed to transmit the messages God sent to Him to the length and breadth of the earth so as many who could hear the voice of God, would be saved. For many years of working as a Prophet of God, he has kept a clean sheet so as a sign of his calling, and also, as an example for all. With five children and grand children, the family of Prophet Francis Kwateng has been a peaceful one and his marriage a prosperous one.
