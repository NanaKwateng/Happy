About the Church

Foundation.

On the 31st December, 2024, House of Power Ministries International, a church founded and led by Prophet Francis Kwateng, with his wife, Mrs. Mercy Kwateng, which was set about in Techiman about 30 years ago, celebrates a grand anniversary of years of righteousness. Starting with just about ten unstable members due to the teachings and doctrines given to the prophet by God, it has now over hundreds of branches both locally and internationally, over a million population and still counting. The church of God is still flourishing gracefully. Headquartered in Ahofadiekrom, the church has branch pastors at every branch to shepherd the sheep of the Living God. The power to be righteous to make heaven the priority of its members has meandered into every heart and ears that hears the message of the founder (Prophet Francis Kwateng). It extends to those who are outside the walls and have the will to join and follow the prophet.
